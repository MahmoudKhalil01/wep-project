About Us

Amazon is a global leader in e-commerce, offering a wide range of products and services to customers all over the world. Our mission is to be Earth's most customer-centric company, where customers can find and discover anything they might want to buy online.

Founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos, Amazon has grown from an online bookstore into a diverse marketplace with a presence in numerous sectors including technology, entertainment, and cloud computing.

Our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction drives everything we do, and we continuously strive to enhance our services and expand our reach.
